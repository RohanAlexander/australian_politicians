  Rev Hon Dr Lynn Arnold AO

PERSONAL DETAILS

Full Name Lynn Maurice Ferguson Arnold AO
QualificationsAO (2004), PhD, BA, BEd
Prior EmploymentTeacher
Public ActivitiesElected to Ramsay in 1985, he was variously Minister for Industry, Trade and Technology, Agriculture, Fisheries and Ethnic Affairs. He became Premier in 1992 following Bannon's resignation, but the political aftermath of the State Bank disaster made it virtually impossible for Labor to retain government. He continued as Leader of the Opposition after Labor's electoral defeat by the Liberals in 1993, and was elected as member for Taylor in the same year before leaving politics in 1994. Presently CEO of World Vision Australia.
