PERSONAL DETAILS

Full Name Patricia Lynne White
QualificationsBE BA FAICD FIEAust
EducationTrish White was born in Queensland and educated at the University of Queensland and University of South Australia. She holds degrees in Engineering and Arts.
Other InformationTrish worked for nine years as an electronics engineer, most recently with the Defence Science and Technology Organisation where she specialised in communications networks.

Trish has two son and a daughter.
Retention of the prefix title Honourable was granted by Her Excellency the Governor on 25 May 2005 in the Govt Gazette of 2 June 2005.
