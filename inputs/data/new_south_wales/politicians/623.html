Qualifications, occupations and interests
Publisher. Educated by his father. Arrived in Sydney with parents in November 1840. Apprenticed to a printer at age 11 and ran away to sea becoming a cabin boy. Went to Turon and Fryer's Creek gold rushes. Worked for 3 years in the government printing office. Became a newspaper publisher and a share broker and agent. Became a police magistrate at Berrima in 1871. Became a bankrupt in 1872. Mining speculator and land agent from 1870's. Director of Wickham and Bullock Island Coal Co. Ltd; Joined Orange Lodge 1867. Estate valued at £14,816.

Personal
Son of John Garrett and Sarah Stafford. Married (1) Mary Ann Craigon and had issue, 3 sons. Married (2) Marcia Grocott and had issue, 1 son. Married (3) Elizabeth McPhillamy and had issue, 2 sons. Church of England.
