Mr Francis Edward MCLEAN (1863 - 1926)

Date of Birth: 01/01/1863
Place of Birth: Sydney, New South Wales, Australia
Date of Death: 04/04/1926
Place of Death: Springwood, New South Wales, Australia

Political Party Activity
Community Activity
Fellow of Sydney Institute of Chartered Accountants. President of Cook Ward Ratepayers Association 1890. Free Trade and Library Associations Office Holder. Australian Natives Association.
Qualifications, occupations and interests
Accounts clerk and timber merchant. Educated in public schools to primary school level. Junior clerk at Mort's dock. Entered service of John Taylor & Co,. Timber merchants in 1879. Started timber business on own account; ruined by depression. Became chief clerk and accountant for firm of W.S Preddy.
Military Service
Honours Received
Membership of other Parliaments & Offices Held
Member of the Parliament of the Commonwealth of Australia. Member of the House of Representatives for Lang 1901 - 1903.
Local Government Activity
Personal
Son of Francis McLean and Elizabeth Pinknell. Married Alice. Methodist.
