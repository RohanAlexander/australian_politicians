Slack, Douglas John (Doug)

Personal


Birth Date: 5 April 1941 (Gayndah, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Cyril Edward Slack and Elsie May (nee Anderson)

Family: Sandra Majorie Guy on 17 June 1967, 4 sons

Education: Primary correspondence; Barambah Creek School; Gayndah Primary; Church of England Grammar School

Religion: Church of England

Career

Farmer/Cattleman/Businessman

Party Positions:

  1. (NP) Former State President, Young National Party
  2. (NP) Chairman, Burnett Electorate Council
  3. (NP) Chairman, Hinkler Divisional Council
  4. (NP) State Vice-President, Wide Bay Zone
  5. (NP) Chairman, Ministerial Legislative Committee: Education
  6. (NP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Youth and Sport; Primary Industries; Finance; Land Management; Environment; Conservation and Tourism; Water Resources and Maritime Services
  7. (NP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committee: Family Services and Welfare Housing
