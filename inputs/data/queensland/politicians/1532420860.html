Lane, Donald Frederick (Don)

Personal


Birth Date: 18 July 1935 (Toowoomba, Queensland, Australia)

Death Date: 11 March 1995

Parents: Frederick James Lane and Mary (nee Bentley)

Family: Beryl Rose Pankhurst on 28 April 1962, 1 son, 1 daughter

Education: State Industrial High School, Brisbane

Religion: Church of England

Career

Detective Senior Constable of Police; Member, Queensland Police Force, 1952 to 1971

Party Positions:

  1. (LIB) Chairman, East Ashgrove Branch
  2. (LIB) Chairman, Brisbane Area Executive (Federal)
  3. (LIB) Former Parliamentary Delegate, Central Campaign Committee
  4. (LIB) Member, Liberal Parliamentary Committee on City of Brisbane Redistribution (Ward Boundaries)
  5. (LIB) Chairman, Metropolitan Government Members Town Planning Committee
  6. (LIB) Member, State Executive, 1969 - 1975
  7. (LIB) Former Member, Liberal Party Executive, 1969 - 1975
  8. (LIB) Member, Government Party Committees: Labour Relations; Welfare; Transport; Industry and Administrative Services; Justice, 1971 - 1980
  9. (LIB) Member, Government Party Committees: Justice; Community and Welfare Services; Transport; Labour Relations and Consumer Affairs; Industrial Development; Urban and Regional Affairs, 1975 - 1977
  10. (NP) Member, Central Council, National Party, 1984 - 1985

Parliamentary Representation:

House Party Electorate From To Elected/Departure Reason

Assembly NP Merthyr 25 Oct 1983 25 Nov 1988
Assembly IND Merthyr 25 Nov 1988 30 Jan 1989
