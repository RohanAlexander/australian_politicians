Rose, Merri

Personal


Birth Date: 24 January 1955 (Kilcoy, Queensland, Australia)

Family: 2 sons

Career

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Former holder of various branch and FDE positions
  2. (ALP) Delegate, ALP State Conference
  3. (ALP) Member, Shadow Ministerial Legislation Committees: Business, Industry and Transport; Tourism, Sport and Recreation and Racing; Environment and Heritage, Mar 1996 - Jun 1998
  4. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Education; Environment and Heritage; Tourism, Sport and Racing, Sep 1992 - Jul 1995
  5. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Tourism, Sport and Youth; Education; Environment and Heritage; Emergency Services and Consumer Affairs, Sep 1995 - Feb 1996
