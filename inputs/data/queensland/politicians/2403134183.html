Palaszczuk, Hon Heinrich (Henry)

Personal


Birth Date: 12 January 1947 (Germany)

Parents: Hipolit Palaszczuk and Ludwika (nee Boba)

Family: 4 daughters

Education: Darr Canvent; St Edmunds Ipswich; Kelvin Grove Teachers College; Queensland University

Religion: Roman Catholic

Relationship to Politician: Henry's daughter Annastacia is the Member for Inala, 2006-

Career

Schoolteacher

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Secretary, Opposition Primary Industry Committee in 45th Parliament
  2. (ALP) Campaign Director, Brisbane Municipal Campaign 1985, 1988, 1991 and 1994
  3. (ALP) Campaign Director, Rankin Federal Divisional Electorate, 1990 and 1993
  4. (ALP) Delegate, ALP State Conference, 1984, 1988, 1992 and 1994
  5. (ALP) Secretary, Inala Branch ALP, 1980 - 1987
  6. (ALP) President, Inala Metropolitan Executive Committee, 1982 - 1986
  7. (ALP) President, Archerfield Electorate Executive Committee, 1982 - 1986
  8. (ALP) President, Rankin Federal Divisional Executive, 1984 - 1986
  9. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Health; Tourism; Environment; Valuation and Administrative Services; National Parks; Sport; the Arts, 1984 - 1989
  10. (ALP) Delegate, ALP State Council, 1984 - 1991
  11. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Treasury; Primary Industries, Sep 1992 - Jul 1995
  12. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Treasury; Police and Corrective Services; Minerals and Energy, Sep 1995 - Feb 1996

Parliamentary Representation:
