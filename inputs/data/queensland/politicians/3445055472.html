Harper, Robert Malcolm (Bob)

Personal


Birth Date: 17 November 1944 (Brisbane, Queensland, Australia)

Death Date: 2 November 2017

Parents: Neville Barclay Harper and Hazel Jane (nee Gardner)

Family: Rhonda, 3 daughters

Education: Bachelor of Business-Accountancy

Career

Accountant in the electricity industry; Member, Australian Society of CPAs

Party Positions:

  1. (LIB) Previously branch Chairman, Secretary and Treasurer
  2. (LIB) Treasurer and Development Officer, Ryan Federal Electorate Council (and zone)
  3. (LIB) Member, Campaign Committees (Federal/State/Council)
  4. (LIB) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committees: Treasurer, Mines and Energy; Tourism, Small Business and Industry, Apr 1996 - Jun 1998
  5. (LIB) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committee: Police and Corrective Services, Apr 1997 - Jun 1998

Parliamentary Representation:
