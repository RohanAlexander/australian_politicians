Bennett, Neil Patrick

Personal


Birth Date: 28 February 1958 (Bundaberg, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Patrick James Bennett and Noela Edith (nee Barrett)

Family: Judy Coxhead on 28 July 1983, 2 sons (twins), 1 daughter

Education: Walkervale Primary and Kepnock High Schools; Qualified electrical fitter/mechanic

Religion: Baptist

Career

Qualified electrical fitter/mechanic; Power station operator

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) Secretary/Treasurer and Treasurer, Gladstone branch
  2. (ALP) Secretary/Treasurer and delegate, Port Curtis Electoral Executive Committee
  3. (ALP) Delegate, Federal Divisional Executive
  4. (ALP) Member, Ministerial Legislation Committees: Business, Industry and Regional Development; Minerals and Energy; Local Government, 1992 - 1995
