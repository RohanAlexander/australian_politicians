FitzGerald, Andrew Anthony (Tony)

Personal


Birth Date: 22 February 1940 (Gatton, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Andrew George FitzGerald and Janet Mary (nee Trevethan)

Family: Patricia Bernadette Daly on 9 May 1964, 3 sons, 1 daughter

Education: Gatton Convent School: Downlands, Toowoomba

Religion: Roman Catholic

Career

Farmer

Party Positions:

  1. (NP) Chairman, Lockyer Electoral Council
  2. (NP) Secretary, Lockyer Electoral Council
  3. (NP) Branch Chairman, Gatton
  4. (NP) Member, Ministerial Legislative Committee: Justice and Attorney-General, Apr 1996 - Jun 1998
