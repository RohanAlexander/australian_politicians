Nelson, Beryce Ann

Personal


Birth Date: 10 January 1947 (Brisbane, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Arthur Lloyd Noble and Thelma Ann (nee Kelso)

Family: John Arthur Nelson on 24 August 1968, 1 son, 2 daughters

Education: Toowoomba and Brisbane state and Brisbane secondary schools; Diploma of Radiography, 1965 to 1967; University of Queensland, Arts degree, 1979

Religion: Roman Catholic

Career

Public relations officer, Down's Syndrome Association, 1979 to 1980; Radiographer in Brisbane and Sydney, 1967 to 1971

Party Positions:

  1. (LIB) Executive member, Liberal Party Women's Council
  2. (LIB) Chairman, Aspley West Branch
  3. (LIB) Vice Chairman, Petrie Federal Division
  4. (LIB) President, Liberal Party Speakers Group (Queensland)
  5. (LIB) Member, Queensland Liberal Party State Executive
  6. (LIB) Secretary, Queensland Liberal Party Health Committee
  7. (LIB) Member, Government Party Committees: Treasury; Employment and Labour Relations; Environment, Valuation and Administrative Services, 1981 - 1983
  8. (NP) Member, Government Party Committees: Premier, Treasurer and the Arts; Employment, Training and Industrial Affairs; Environment, Conservation and Tourism; Justice and Attorney-General, 1987 - Sep 1989
  9. (NP) Member, Government Party Committees: Tourism, National Parks and Sport; Employment, Small Business and Industrial Affairs; Health and Environment, 1987
