Welford, Hon Rodney Jon (Rod)

Personal


Birth Date: 30 September 1958 (Brisbane, Queensland, Australia)

Parents: Douglas Welford and Gwendolyn May

Family: Rosemary Anne Walters on 4 January 1875, 1 daughter

Education: Aspley East State Primary School; St Paul's Anglican Grammar School; Bachelor of Arts (First Class Honours); Bachelor of Laws; Graduate Diploma in Legal Practice; Graduate Diploma in Industrial Relations; Master of Science in Environmental Management, 2001

Religion: Church of England

Career

Solicitor, Supreme Court of Queensland; Barrister, High Court of Australia

Party Positions:

  1. (ALP) President, Aspley West Chermside Branch, 1982 - 1983
  2. (ALP) Secretary, Petrie FDE, 1987 - 1988
  3. (ALP) Aspley-Zillmere Branch, 1987
  4. (ALP) President, Aspley-Zillmere Branch, 1988 - 1989
  5. (ALP) Delegate, State Conference, 1988 - 1991
  6. (ALP) State Council, 1988 - 1991
  7. (ALP) Member, State Rules Committee, 1989 - 1992
  8. (ALP) Member, State Laws and Law Enforcement Committee, 1989 - 1992

Parliamentary Representation:

House Party Electorate From To Elected/Departure Reason
Assembly ALP Stafford 2 Dec 1989 19 Sep 1992
Assembly ALP Everton 19 Sep 1992 20 Mar 2009 Did not contest

Speeches: All Speeches
