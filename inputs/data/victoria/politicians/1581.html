Barry Edward Hector Steggall

RecordNo
1581
Parents
Norman Henry, primary producer, and Dorothy Horsefall
Marriage

Religion: Church of England

Marital Status: 29 January 1972, Suzanne Margaret Harvey; 1s. 2d.

Education
Canterbury, Fish Point, Swan Hill State Schools, Swan Hill High School; Gordon Institute of Technology, Certificate of Wool Classing 1960-62.
Career

Woolclasser throughout Australia 1962-65. Worked in wool industry, Bradford and London 1965-66, and with International Wool Secretariat in Chile 1967. Primary producer on irrigation and dry land country (merino sheep & cereals) 1968-83. APEX member 1968-1980, president 1971-1972, life member 1980. Chairman, Swan Hill Sewerage Authority 1974-78.

Farmer

Swan Hill city councillor 1973-1983, mayor 1980-1982.
Party
National Party
