Surname: POLLEY
Given Names: Michael Robert
Title and Honours: Mr, Hon (12 May 1992)
Qualifications:
Date and Place of Birth: 4 November 1949 - Westbury, Tasmania.
Date of Death:

House of Assembly: 22 April 1972
Electorate: Wilmot/Lyons
Party: ALP
Positions Held: Minister 1976-80; Speaker 1989-92; 1998 - 2014
Date of Departure: 14 March 2014
Reason for Departure: Retired

Inaugural Speech
Comments: Son-in-law of ALP MHA Geoff Chisholm.
Youngest Minister ever appointed (27).
While serving as Speaker of the House, his sister Helen Polley was elected Senator, wife Kim Polley was elected Northern Midlands Council Mayor, and son Tim was elected to the Northern Midlands Council. Granted the right to use the title 'Honourable' for life on 12 May 1992.
