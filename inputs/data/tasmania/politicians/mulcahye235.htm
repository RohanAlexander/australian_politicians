Surname: MULCAHY
Given Names: Edward
Title and Honours: Mr
Qualifications:
Date and Place of Birth: 28 March 1850 - Limerick, England
Date of Death: 23 October 1927 - Hobart, Tasmania

House of Assembly: (1) 19 August 1891 (by-election) (2) 20 January 1897 (3) 25 October 1899 (4) 8 June 1910 (by-election) (5) 7 November 1914
Electorate: (1) West Hobart (2) Hobart (3) Hobart (4) Wilmot (Lyons) (5) Wilmot (Lyons)
Party: Anti-Socialist to 1912; Liberal to 1920.
Positions Held: Chair Public Works Committee
Minister: Yes
Date of Departure: (1) 20 January 1897 (2) 25 October 1899 (3) 2 April 1903 (4) 23 July 1914 (5) 15 January 1919
Reason for Departure: (1) Seat abolished. (2) Resigned on appointment to Ministry. (3) Defeated. (4) Resigned and re-elected. (5) Resigned to take up appointment to Senate.

Comments: During early business career he established various hotels and Gaiety Theatre in Zeehan.
