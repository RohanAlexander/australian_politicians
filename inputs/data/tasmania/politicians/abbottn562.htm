The Parliament of Tasmania from 1856

Surname: ABBOTT
Given Names: Nigel Drury Gresley
Title and Honours: Dr, Honourable
Qualifications: MB, BS, LLB
Date and Place of Birth: 29 March 1920 - Clunes, Victoria
Marriage: 27 August 1948 - (Jocelyn Butler)
Date of Death: 13 November 2011

House of Assembly: 2 May 1964
Electorate: Denison
Party: Liberal
Positions Held: Minister 1969-72
Date of Departure: 22 April 1972
Reason for Departure: Defeated.

Comments: Resigned from Government to stand as independent after failing to get road safety measures through. Served in Vietnam as Head of a Military Aid Mission for three months in 1968. Examiner, 23 May 1968. Granted the right to use the title 'Honourable' for life on 15 April 1991.
