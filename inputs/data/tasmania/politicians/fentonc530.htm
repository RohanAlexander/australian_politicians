The Parliament of Tasmania from 1856

Surname: FENTON
Given Names: Charles Balfour Marcus
Title and Honours: Mr, OA (1982), Honourable
Qualifications:
Date and Place of Birth: 17 January 1912 - Smithton, Tasmania
Date of Death: 12 June 2009

Legislative Council: 11 May 1957
Electorate: Russell
Party: Independent
Positions Held: Chair Committees 1971-72; President 7 June 1972 - 9 June 1981
Date of Departure: 23 May 1981
Reason for Departure: Retired.

Comments: Succeeded his uncle A.B. Fenton. Re-elected unopposed 1969. As President, he was made a member of the Executive Council on 3 December 1975 to commemorate 150th anniversary of first sitting of EC. Granted the right to use the title 'Honourable' for life on 21 July 1981. Condolence Motion in LC 16 June 2009.
