Surname: GRAY
Given Names: Robin Trevor
Title and Honours: Mr, Honourable
Qualifications: BAgrSc
Date and Place of Birth: 1 March 1940 - Kew, Victoria
Date of Death:

House of Assembly: 11 December 1976
Electorate: Wilmot/Lyons
Party: Liberal
Positions Held: Opposition Leader 11 November 1981 to 27 May 1982; 29 June 1989 to 17 December 1991
Minister: Premier 26 May 1982 to 29 June 1989; various other portfolios.
Date of Departure: 1 December 1995
Reason for Departure: Retired.

Comments: Granted the right to use the title 'Honourable' for life on 4 August 1992.
