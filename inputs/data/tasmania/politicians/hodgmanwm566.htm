Given Names: William Michael (Michael)
Title and Honours: Mr, QC, Hon
Qualifications: LLB, QC
Date and Place of Birth: 16 November 1938, Hobart Tasmania
Date of Death: 19 June 2013

Legislative Council: 14 May 1966
Electorate: Huon
Party: Independent
Positions Held:
Date of Departure: 25 May 1974
Reason for Departure: Resigned to contest Denison (Federal). Successful.

House of Assembly: (1) 1 February 1992 (2) 21 August 2001
Electorate: Denison
Party: Liberal
Positions Held: Shadow Attorney General & Shadow Minister for Justice (August 2001 - September 2009) Shadow Minister for Veterans Affairs (August 2001 - date).

Date of Departure: (1) 29 August 1998 (2) 20 March 2010
Reason for Departure: (1) Defeated. (2) Retired.

Inaugural Speech


Comments: Son of WC Hodgman QC, MHA, MLC; brother of Peter Hodgman MLC, MHA; father of Will Hodgman MP. Elected second time on recount after retirement of Ray Groom MHA. Granted the right to use the title 'Honourable' for life on 3 November 1980.
